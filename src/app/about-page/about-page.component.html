About Us
Our Purpose

At Coco Casing, our purpose is crystal clear - to offer phone cases that not only look good but also protect your cherished device. We understand the pivotal role your phone plays in your daily life, and we go the extra mile to ensure it remains safeguarded from the rigors of everyday use.

Our commitment to delivering high-quality cases, made from top-tier materials, ensures that your phone stays safe from bumps and scratches. We take pride in providing you with a range of choices that reflect your personality while offering the durability your phone deserves.
In essence, our purpose is to seamlessly integrate style, practicality, and protection into every Coco Casing phone case, all while fostering a sense of trust and satisfaction with our valued customers.

Our Roots

Coco Casing is a phone case company with a distinctive approach. We firmly believe that phone cases should seamlessly blend practicality with style, and our designs are a testament to this philosophy. Offering an array of colors and designs, we empower you to discover the perfect case that aligns with your unique personality.

Founded in Sweden, Coco Casing is a brand under the umbrella of Shadida Trading Company AB, a family-owned business. Our company is deeply committed to delivering innovative, straightforward, and equitable products to our valued customers.
Nimer & Nadi, the driving forces behind our brand, bring a wealth of professional experience from the Electro-, Automation-, and IT-consultancy industry. With over a decade of experience as operational business owners in diverse sectors such as retail, food, tech, and the Swedish startup landscape, we have honed our ability to consistently deliver high-quality products. Our focus remains steadfastly on understanding and prioritizing the needs of our customers while cultivating authentic, enduring relationships.

In the realm of mobile phone cases, this commitment shines through our rigorous quality assurance practices, our strong relationships with trusted suppliers, and our dedication to providing you, our valued visitor, with accessible and efficient communication channels.
